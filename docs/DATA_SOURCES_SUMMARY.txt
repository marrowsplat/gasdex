GASDEX DATA SOURCES RESEARCH — SUMMARY
========================================

RESEARCH COMPLETED: July 19, 2026

KEY FINDINGS:
=============

1. PRIMARY RECOMMENDATION: API-Football (api-sports.io)
   - Free tier: 100 requests/day (no credit card needed)
   - Coverage: 1,200+ leagues including EFL League Two
   - All endpoints available on free tier (no league gating)
   - Cost: $0 free or $19/month if volume exceeds
   - Verdict: BEST FOR GASDEX — reliable, low-friction, proven coverage

2. FALLBACK: football-data.org
   - Free tier: 12 major leagues (NO League Two)
   - Paid: €49/month for 25 leagues (League Two available but unconfirmed)
   - Rate limit: Very generous (10 req/min = 600/hour)
   - Verdict: Reliable fallback if API-Football fails; costs more

3. AVOID:
   - SportMonks: €99/month minimum (too expensive for hobby)
   - FotMob: No official API (ToS violation risk, fragile)
   - BBC/scraping: Explicit ToS prohibition on automation
   - TheSportsDB: Community data quality (secondary only)
