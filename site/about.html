About GasDex

About GasDex

GasDex is an unofficial, fan-run home page for Bristol Rovers — everything worth knowing about the Gas, gathered onto one fast page. News from every outlet, official club announcements, YouTube highlights and podcasts, results, fixtures, and the bits the fans make themselves: match reports from the terraces and player ratings after every game.

How it works

The site looks after itself. Feeds are checked and the page rebuilds automatically every few hours, around the clock, whether anyone's watching or not. There's no editorial team and no publishing schedule — if something Rovers-related happens, it shows up here shortly afterwards. The one human touch: fan match reports are read by an actual person before they're published.

The fan content

After every match, fans can rate the players (one ballot per fan, closes 48 hours after full time) and submit a match report. Ratings are aggregated and published automatically; reports are reviewed first — see the house rules on the submission page. Over a season these build into something no other site has: a running record of what it was actually like to follow Bristol Rovers, written by the people who were there.

Should we include something?

If there's a Rovers site, podcast, or channel we should be listing — or a feed has gone quiet — drop us a line. The links directory is the one bit of the site a human curates, and recommendations are always welcome.

The small print

GasDex is not affiliated with Bristol Rovers FC. All club names and trademarks belong to their owners. Match data via TheSportsDB. Headlines and videos link to their original publishers — we aggregate, we don't republish. Fan reports are the views of their authors, not of GasDex (though between us, they're usually right).
